My Favorite Games

Dmc Devil May Cry

A fast-paced action game that follows Dante's journey battling demons with stylish combos and epic boss fights. Known for its high difficulty and dynamic gameplay.

Dark Souls 3

A dark fantasy action RPG famous for its challenging combat, atmospheric world, and deep lore. Players must master dodging, timing, and perseverance to survive in a ruined and haunting universe.

League of Legends

A highly popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game with a huge roster of champions. Strategic team fights and fast-paced action dominate the gameplay.

Little Nightmares

A suspenseful puzzle-platformer with eerie atmosphere where you guide a small girl named Six through a mysterious and dangerous world filled with grotesque creatures.

Outlast 2

A terrifying survival horror game set in a rural area with a dark mystery. Players must hide and run from threats, relying on a night-vision camera to survive.

Hollow Knight

A beautifully animated indie Metroidvania game that takes place in the mysterious underground kingdom of Hallownest. With tight platforming, intricate combat, and a haunting soundtrack, it's both tough and emotionally impactful.

Zelda Ocarina Of Time

An iconic action-adventure game where you play as Link, a young hero destined to save the land of Hyrule. Known for its deep story, puzzles, and time-travel mechanics, it's one of the most influential games of all time.

Persona 5

A stylish Japanese RPG where you balance high school life by day and fight supernatural enemies by night. With a unique art style, catchy soundtrack, and powerful story about rebellion and identity, it’s one of the most beloved modern JRPGs.
